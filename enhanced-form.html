About KidCOde

KidCOde is a platform designed to help children learn coding in a fun and interactive way.

Our events are tailored for kids aged 8 to 15, providing a safe and engaging environment to explore the world of programming.

Our upcoming event is set to happen on September 15, 2025.

Activities

We have a number of activities designed to engage and inspire young coders

Our activities include:

  • Basic HTML and CSS
  • Problem-solving through coding games
  • Building a mini web page

We have merch and awards for participants!
